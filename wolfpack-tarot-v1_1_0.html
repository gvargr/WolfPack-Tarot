How to use
Overview
The Tarot Prompt Library generates Stable Diffusion image prompts by combining your characters with randomly drawn tarot cards. Each card contributes its own visual symbolism and thematic energy to the prompt, producing three unique outputs in a Past · Present · Future spread.
Steps
01Select your checkpoint family at the top. This changes the quality prefix tags and art style language in the output to match Illustrious / NoobAI-XL, Pony Diffusion, or Anima.
02Toggle SFW / NSFW to unlock the appropriate rating tiers. NSFW mode must be enabled before R or X rated outputs become available.
03Set up your three character slots. Switch each slot between Custom OC (paste any tag string or full prompt) and Random (picks from 22 anime archetypes with a Reroll option).
04On Random slots, choose a per-card content rating. SFW mode offers PG and PG-R. NSFW mode offers R and X.
05Press Draw Three Cards. Three cards are drawn at random from the full 78-card deck. Each card may be upright or reversed (25% chance). Reversed cards add shadowed and disrupted imagery to the prompt.
06Copy the positive and negative prompts from each result card and paste them into your SD frontend of choice.

Tips
Custom OC prompts can be a full positive prompt string or just a tag list describing your character. The tool merges them cleanly with the card imagery either way. You do not need to include quality boosters in your custom prompt as the selected model prefix handles those.
Suit-specific art style language is applied automatically per card. Cups outputs lean into watercolor and emotional depth, Wands get fire and dynamism, Swords get storm and sharp lines, Pentacles get earthy texture and botanical detail.
